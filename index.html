About Me

I am a Master's student in Mechanical Engineering at UC San Diego with a strong passion for robotics, mechatronics, and intelligent systems. My research centers on integrating mechanical design, circuit design, and intelligent control to develop robotic platforms that can be applied in both general automation and biomedical/medical robotics, addressing real-world challenges that improve efficiency and human well-being.

With over four years of leadership experience in international competitions and technical project management, I have cultivated both engineering expertise and team coordination skills. I am proficient in Python, C/C++, MATLAB, ROS, YOLO, CAD/PCB design (Inventor, Autodesk, Fusion360, EasyEDA), and rapid prototyping techniques such as CNC machining, 3D printing, and laser cutting.

My long-term goal is to advance in the fields of robotics and medical robotics, leveraging interdisciplinary knowledge to design innovative solutions that enhance healthcare, industry, and everyday life.
