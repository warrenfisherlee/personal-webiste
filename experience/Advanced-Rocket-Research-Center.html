Advanced Rocket Research Center

As a Propulsion Intern, I developed and implemented the ignition system and oxygen flow control piping using pneumatic valves to support hot-fire testing. I tested and analyzed 300 N O₂ hybrid rocket engines to optimize satellite propulsion performance and integrated a data-acquisition (DAQ) system to automate engine testing while capturing pressure and thrust data for performance analysis. Additionally, I collaborated with rocket engineers during engine assembly to ensure safe integration and test readiness.
