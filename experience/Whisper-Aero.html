Whisper Aero

As a Mechanical Engineering Intern, I led the design and integration of an automated Class 3 laser balancing enclosure system to support scalable manufacturing and developed Python-based automation for laser alignment and motor control, achieving G2.5-grade precision in electric blisk balancing. I tested titanium and aluminum components to optimize galvo fiber laser alignment, reducing balancing time to under five minutes, and designed and built a custom electrical cabinet by performing power distribution and thermal calculations to size breakers and manage a 150W heat load, enabling over 100 test cycles without overheating. Additionally, I prototyped GD&T-compliant metal tooling rigs to support precise assembly and testing of UAV hardware.
