Boeing Space Based Detection CubeSa

As a Structures Engineer on Boeing’s Space-Based Debris Detection CubeSat project, I developed the 12U CubeSat CAD model for a LEO mission, leading structural design efforts to ensure compliance with mission requirements. I performed FEA and bolted joint analyses under launch loads to secure the electro-optical sensor, and conducted modal and vibration analyses up to 1000 g to maintain optical alignment over a 3+ year mission lifespan. To validate system performance, I designed an optomechanical testing system and precision servo-controlled camera stand to simulate debris tracking with a rotating laser, confirming alignment for detection of debris smaller than 10 cm. I also presented intermediate designs at PDRs and CDRs with Boeing engineers, incorporating feedback to efficiently finalize structural solutions.
