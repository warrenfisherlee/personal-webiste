Advanced Rocket Research Center

As a Research Assistant, I designed and built a closed-loop water-rig to model transverse jet mixing; used a laser sheet and fluorescein dye for flow visualization, captured vortex dynamics with high-speed imaging, and processed data in MATLAB/Python to quantify mixing length, vortex timescales, and jet impingement behavior. Developed experimental protocols to replicate rocket chamber flow conditions, enabling validation of transverse-injection concepts for improved combustion stability and reduced thermal loading.
