Robotics Club Leader

I served as the 12th President of DIT Robotics, a team of approximately 60 undergraduate students at National Tsing Hua University. Founded with the motto “Do, Improve, and Try,” DIT emphasizes applying classroom knowledge through hands-on projects, continuous improvement, and experimentation, with a strong competition-oriented focus in both autonomous and remote-controlled robotics contests. As president, I acted as both the final decision-maker and project manager (PM), overseeing project timelines, ensuring tasks were completed on schedule, and supporting team members when needed. Under my leadership, the team achieved 2nd place at ASME SPDC 2024 Taiwan, and led two teams to compete in Eurobot 2024 International, earning 5th place, 9th place, and the Innovation Award.
