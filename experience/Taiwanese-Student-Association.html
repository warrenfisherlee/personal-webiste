Taiwanese Student Association

I led the marketing team by filming and editing promotional videos, writing scripts, and managing social media outreach to maximize event visibility. Additionally, I collaborated with fellow officers on event logistics, volunteer coordination, and budget planning, while mentoring new members and strengthening cross-cultural communication and leadership skills.
