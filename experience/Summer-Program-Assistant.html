Summer Program Assistnat

As a Summer Program Assistant, I led lectures on engineering statics and facilitated hands-on workshops in CAD modeling, 3D printing, and laser cutting. I mentored ~90 high school students during a four-week engineering academy, supporting team-based design projects from concept to prototype. I developed and delivered lesson plans, supervised lab activities to ensure safety and technical accuracy, and provided one-on-one guidance to help students strengthen problem-solving skills and apply engineering principles. In addition, I collaborated with faculty and other assistants to coordinate curriculum delivery, ensuring participants gained both technical knowledge and exposure to real-world engineering practices.(https://www.chronline.com/stories/in-focus-students-make-a-splash-at-university-of-washington-stem-camp-at-wf-west-in-chehalis,385301)
