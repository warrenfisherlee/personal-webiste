2022 TEL Robot Combat Mechanisms

In the 2022 TEL Robot Combat, which combined the Smart Track and Treasure Battle Arena, my teammates and I co-designed the robot’s mechanism to balance stability and flexibility, enabling efficient responsiveness and precise performance in both autonomous navigation and manual control.

SCARA Arm

The SCARA arm offers high precision and a wide operational range, with vertical motion driven by a large-pitch ball screw to improve speed. To enable stacking of three blocks, sufficient vertical travel is required; due to structural constraints, the end-effector employs a T-shaped suction cup assembly with three different lengths. The longer cup handles smaller objects, while the shortest cup is used for stacking the third block, ensuring both accuracy and space efficiency.

Mecanum Wheel Chassis

The mecanum wheel chassis, powered by four DC motors, enables omnidirectional mobility. In the first half of the competition, it allows rapid lateral movement, particularly advantageous in Stage 3 when maneuvering down a slope within limited space. In the second half, its high maneuverability enables the robot to easily avoid obstacles and quickly align its body for object gripping, significantly improving overall efficiency.
